It is best to wash your hair at least twice a week, no more and no less. Your hair will be softer with masks suitable for your hair type. You should not wash your hair with water that is too hot or too cold. In the evening, it is essential to sleep with your hair down, otherwise it will break. Preferably, you should cut your hair at least once a year. It is essential not to do hairstyles that are too tight. It is essential to reduce or stop using curling irons and straighteners. You should not brush your hair aggressively. And above all, do not overuse bleaching.
